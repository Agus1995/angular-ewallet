  • What is Forex Trading?

    Forex trading is a business activity, investment, and even a profession. Forex trading is trading currencies from different countries with the aim of making a profit

  • Forex Market

    The forex market is different from traditional markets. Because here the trade is a currency, the market (where the traders / market players make buying and selling) is not a particular building, and every market actor can play a dual role as a seller and buyer. Who are these forex market players? very diverse: it can be multinational banks, central banks, large companies, government of any country, financial institutions, speculators, etc.

  • Risk of Forex Trading

    Apart from various advantages, forex trading can be likened to a double-edged sword. Forex trading can make us rich, but also can instantly erode our capital. No matter whether you consider forex as one type of investment or as a normal trade, it is clear that forex trading risk is high.
